Switching On and Off



The Tamatar are switched on whenever there is no plug inserted. As soon as the Tamatar detect either the switch-off-, or charging plug, they will automatically go to sleep.

Make sure that all Tamatar have the switch-off plug inserted during the days when Kunsthalle is closed and overnight!

 

Charging

First, connect the Tamatar to the charging station through the orange wires. You only need the stop-button (left) and the start-button (right) on the charging device.


1) Charger must be set to the program on the picture. Use the stop-button to switch to the correct program.


2) Press the start-button until 'BATTERY CHECK' appears on the screen. This may take 5 seconds.

3) If the battery check is done, the charger asks for confirmation again. Press the start-button to begin


4) If all went well, the charger starts and displays status information about the progress.

 

WARNING

If the charger displays a 'LOW VOLTAGE' message, the battery is discharged beyond the allowed level. You need to open the Tamatar and replace the battery! Replacement batteries can be found in the etoy storage container in the grey box. Ask the available etoy.AGENTS for help, or contact silvan@etoy.com.

 

Maintainance 2nd battery

Every second or third week, the Tamatar need to recharge their second batteries. These are mounted on the chassis, therefore the styrofoam sphere needs to be opened. It should be sufficient to open the opposite side of the charger plug (the side with only one hole at the center). After removing one half of the styrofoam sphere, you should see the battery pack underneath the chassis protected with tape.


1) The second battery pack underneath the chassis.


2) Remove the tape.


3) Take out the three batteries from the holder


4) Charge the batteries with the the dedicated NiMh-charger

Charging these batteries takes rather long (~12 hours). Carrying out this maintainance is probably most suitable during the night or on mondays.
